About us

We started with just the Five of us - Fern, Errol, Arjay, Rex, Kathleen and Lea. We are a group of Filipino Freelancers with different skillsets and have similar goals in mind. 

First, we want to have fun in what we're doing.

Second, we want to make use of our talents to gratefully impact the lives of the people around us.

Third, more than the law firms, we want to help people with disability get what they are ethically, legally and medically entitled for by helping the law firms that serves them.

Lastly, setting all laptops and the internet-jungle aside, we have our own personal lives and families which inspires us to be persistent in what we do.

The only thing that would make us stop is when you are satisfied with the work that we do.
