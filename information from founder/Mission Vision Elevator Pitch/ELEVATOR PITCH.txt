60-Second Sales Pitch

Hi, I'm Fernando, founder of Sharpened.

Before starting Sharpened, I worked in Social Security Disability operations, including intake and filing. During that time, I noticed that many teams spent hours each day on repetitive administrative work—following up with clients, tracking signatures, coordinating handoffs between departments, and managing routine communication.

Sharpened was created to solve those operational challenges.

We work exclusively with SSDI law firms to analyze their workflows, identify bottlenecks, and automate repetitive processes without disrupting the systems they already rely on. Whether it's intake, filing, or case management, our goal is to help firms operate more efficiently so their staff can focus on what matters most: helping clients obtain the disability benefits they deserve.
